Interests

As mentioned above, I love working with computers. As a side job/hobby, I build computers for friends and family. My favorite ones to build are gaming computers because they often have a glass windows, RGB lighting, and water cooling systems that really show off the internal components of the computer. This is extra challenging to build because I have to make sure the cables are nicely routed and as invisible as possible when viewing it from the windows. It can be tough at times, but it's always satisfying to see a new PC boot up for the first time. Besides building computers, my other main passions are:
